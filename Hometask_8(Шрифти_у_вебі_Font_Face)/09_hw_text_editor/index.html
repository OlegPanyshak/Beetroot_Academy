Roy Batty, 2019

Like Tears in Rain

Where am I going? How long have I got?

I've seen things you people wouldn't believe. Attack ships on fire off the shoulder of Orion. I watched C-beams glitter in the dark near the Tannhauser gate. All those moments will be lost in time... like tears in the rain... Time to die.

I don't know why he saved my life. Maybe in those last moments he loved life more than ever had before. Notjust his life - anybody's life; my life. All he'd wanted were the same answers...
